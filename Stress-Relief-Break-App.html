Three more quick tools

5-4-3-2-1 grounding ~2 min

Silently name: 5 things you can see, 4 you can hear, 3 you can touch, 2 you can smell, 1 you can taste (or simply your breath). This brings your attention back to the here and now.

Quick body scan ~3 min

Lower your gaze or close your eyes. Mentally sweep your body from your feet to your head, without changing anything, just noticing tension. Finish with three deep breaths.

An anchor phrase ~1 min

Silently repeat a short phrase in rhythm with your breath: inhale "I am here," exhale "this will pass." Pick your own and keep the same one each time.
